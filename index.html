Covid Response Group

Contact tracing is essential for containing any Pandemic. This open-source contact tracing solution uses Bluetooth to identify who you have been with & where you have been to help Governments easily trace an infected person's contact history.

Entire solution is open source and you can follow the development on Github.com

Mobile App

  • Google Sign in
  • Count of people contacted
  • Vulnerability score: Based on the number of infected people within 10 kms radius.

Dashboard

  • Network graph representation for contact tracing
  • Mark infected cases
  • Push circulars and videos to the selected
